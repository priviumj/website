Expert Services

Professional expertise delivering measurable improvements

New Lawn Installation

Complete new lawn services from preparation to establishment. We specialise in Perth's popular varieties including Sir Walter Buffalo, Kikuyu, Couch, and Zoysia. Our installation includes soil preparation, levelling, turf laying, and critical establishment watering schedules tailored to each grass type's specific requirements for successful root development.

Soft Landscaping

Transform your outdoor spaces with our comprehensive soft landscaping services. We specialise in garden bed creation, strategic plant selection for Perth's climate, mulching with stabilisation, decorative gravel installation, and garden edging solutions. Our approach combines aesthetic design with practical maintenance considerations, ensuring your soft landscaping elements enhance your property while remaining manageable year-round.

Seasonal Garden Assessments

Our detailed seasonal assessment reports document your garden's health, track changes over time, and provide specific recommendations. We monitor nutrient levels, pH balance, watering cycles, and mowing heights to ensure optimal garden performance year-round.

Premium Bio-Stimulant Programs

Our bio-stimulant applications use professional-grade liquid products with surfactants that dramatically improve nutrient uptake and soil health. Applied every second visit, these treatments create the thick, lush lawns that set Privium-maintained gardens apart.

Integrated Pest Management

We employ environmentally responsible pest control strategies that protect beneficial insects while effectively managing harmful pests. Our approach includes monitoring, prevention, and targeted treatments only when necessary.

Comprehensive Soil Management

We provide complete soil testing for lawns and garden beds, identifying deficiencies and creating targeted treatment plans. Our soil cultivation, pH adjustment, and nutrient management address the unique challenges of Perth's sandy soils.

Tennis Court Maintenance

Professional maintenance for grass and synthetic tennis courts. Grass court services include precision mowing, line marking, and specialised fertilisation. For synthetic courts, we provide brushing, moss treatment, and infill management to ensure optimal playing conditions year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

During Perth's growing season (September to April), we recommend fortnightly mowing for most lawns. In winter, monthly service is usually sufficient. Premium lawns like Sir Walter Buffalo may benefit from weekly mowing during peak growth periods.

Do you service my suburb?

We service all of Perth's premier suburbs including the Western Suburbs, Riverside areas, and Northern beaches. Check our service area map below or call Jarod on 0490 841 667 to confirm coverage for your specific location.

What's included in your regular maintenance service?

Our standard service includes lawn mowing, edge trimming, hedge maintenance, weeding of garden beds, leaf blowing, and green waste removal. We also provide complimentary lawn health checks and can add fertilising, pest control, and irrigation checks as needed.

Do you remove green waste?

Yes, all green waste removal is included in our service. We take all clippings, prunings, and garden debris with us, leaving your property clean and tidy. For large-scale cleanups, we can arrange additional removal capacity.

Are you insured?

Yes, Privium Garden Services is fully insured with comprehensive public liability coverage and property insurance. We're also registered (ABN: 58 685 448 800) and all our equipment is regularly maintained and safety-checked.

What is your Set & Forget subscription service?

Our subscription model provides scheduled, automatic service with predictable monthly billing via direct debit. You'll receive service reminders, never have to remember to book, and enjoy priority scheduling. It's perfect for busy professionals who want a beautiful garden without the hassle.

Do you offer one-off services?

Yes, we provide one-off services for special occasions, property sales, or seasonal cleanups. However, regular maintenance clients receive priority scheduling and preferential rates. Call us to discuss your specific needs.
