Our Story

Beautiful Gardens for You

Privium Garden Services began with a simple vision: to create and maintain beautiful gardens that enhance how families live and enjoy their outdoor spaces. We understand that gardens aren't just about aesthetics – they're where children play safely, where friends gather for celebrations, and where families create lasting memories. Our approach focuses on delivering these meaningful outcomes through meticulous attention to every detail.

Founded by Jarod, who brings over 10 years of experience in garden care and maintenance, Privium has grown from a single operator to a trusted team serving Perth's most prestigious suburbs.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality, safety, and putting our clients at the centre of everything we do. We don't just cut grass and trim hedges – we assess your garden's needs, apply the right treatments at the right time, and use premium products that transform ordinary gardens into extraordinary outdoor spaces.

Today, we're proud to maintain your properties across Perth, from heritage homes in Cottesloe to modern estates in Dalkeith, each receiving the same level of care and expertise that has become our hallmark.

What Makes Privium Different

Comprehensive Subscription Service

Our subscription service covers everything in your garden ecosystem. While others focus on just mowing, we provide integrated lawn care, tree and shrub maintenance, pest management, irrigation optimisation, and seasonal adjustments. You simply enjoy the results.

Science-Based Garden Care

We offer comprehensive testing and measurement services. If needed, we monitor nutrient and pH levels to create calculated schedules, ensuring every decision is backed by data and expertise. Your garden receives exactly what it needs, when it needs it.

Professional Team & Equipment

Our experienced professionals use specialised equipment like tripod ladders for large hedges, cylinder mowers for golf-cut lawns, and professional-grade application equipment for our premium bio-stimulants and treatments.

Detailed Care & Documentation

We can offer a customised approach with detailed seasonal assessment reports. We document our work, track garden changes, monitor nutrient and pH levels, optimise watering cycles, and provide recommendations for continuous improvement.

Convenience That Works

Our streamlined process handles everything from initial consultation through ongoing service reminders. We provide waste removal, minor irrigation repairs, and even conveniences like fruit collection and bird bath filling - because great garden care is about the details.

The Privium Promise

We don't just maintain gardens - we create exceptional outcomes. Every service is focused on achieving measurable improvements in your garden's health, appearance, and resilience. We take care of everything necessary for superior results, so you can enjoy a garden that's not just maintained, but truly thriving.
