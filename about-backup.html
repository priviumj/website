Our Story

Building Beautiful Gardens Since 2009

Privium Garden Services began with a simple vision: to provide Perth property owners with garden care that goes beyond basic maintenance. We saw a gap in the market for a service that truly listens to clients and delivers tailored solutions with meticulous attention to detail.

Founded by Jarod, who brings over 10 years of experience in horticulture and landscape management, Privium has grown from a single operator to a trusted team serving Perth's most prestigious suburbs.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and understanding what makes gardens thrive. We don't just cut grass and trim hedges – we assess your garden's needs, apply the right treatments at the right time, and use premium products that transform ordinary gardens into extraordinary outdoor spaces.

Today, we're proud to maintain over 500 properties across Perth, from heritage homes in Cottesloe to modern estates in Dalkeith, each receiving the same level of care and expertise that has become our hallmark.
